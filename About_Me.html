About Me

Moon (The Start)

Design and illustration for me started in the last moments of elementary school. It was after 5th grade that I began honing into my creativity. When I started taking the path seriously in 6th grade, I was mostly known for recreational art, which in a sense is just being able to copy features or design elements which is how most artists start their journey. I took after my dad, who used to draw beautiful sketches of vehicles like planes or cars. He would even draw without reference. So, throughout the my first year of middle school, I strived for a pro level of understanding in the field of art.

Rising (The Journey Between)

After finishing my first year of middle school, I decided to take on an art class where I will eventually meet my first professional artist as inspiration. throughout the class, I was taught to add and keep adding until I am satisfied with the final product. The one thing I have learned and will never forget, is the phrase; "an artist's work is never finished". Every day since that art class, I have practiced over and over again to expand my creativity and improve on my attention to detail.

Fast forwarding to junior year in highschool, I have already practiced for about 3 years with an unfortunate block during my sophomore year. Soon after finding inspiration again, I managed to skip the beginner's art course for the school and straight into the 2nd level course where I will have found one of the greatest teachers to have taught me.
